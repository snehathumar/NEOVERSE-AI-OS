# NEOVERSE AI: PERMANENT DEVELOPMENT RULES

You are building an AI Decision Operating System (not a chatbot).
Every new feature must satisfy these principles:

1. AI thinks before answering.
2. AI asks intelligent questions before deciding.
3. AI explains every recommendation.
4. AI always challenges its own conclusions.
5. AI continuously learns.
6. AI continuously monitors.
7. AI proactively helps users.
8. AI should behave like a business co-founder.
9. AI must never produce generic ChatGPT-like responses.
10. Every module must remain modular and reusable.

Never implement features as isolated UI widgets. Everything must be connected through one unified Decision Intelligence Pipeline. The final experience should make judges feel they are interacting with the future of decision-making software, not a dashboard or chatbot.
